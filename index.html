More In Need

Bangladesh, a land of natural beauty and resources, blessed with around 310 rivers. But recently, this blessing turned into bustle when the monsoon started in July. Heavy rainfall started throughout the country and flow of large-scale water leading to massive flooding situation in low lying areas of Northern, north-eastern and south-eastern districts. Now thousands of family is flood affected, lost their houses, livestock, struggling with contagious diseases waiting for support and relief.

Our Donate Plan

we have various kinds of donate plan such as food for the flood affected family,shelter for the flood affected family,education for the flood affected children.

Food For a Family

Safe food and water supplies are a major concern following a natural disaster such as a tornado, flood, hurricane or blizzard. Knowing how to safely handle food and water in the immediate few days after the disaster can reduce stress, worry and inconvenience.

Shelter For a Family

Non-tactical shelter designed to offer an economical solution for humanitarian aid in the event of a natural disaster or conflict worldwide. This shelter is capable of withstanding a steady wind speed of 55 mph with gusts up to 65 mph.

Education for a child

The importance of education cannot be overestimated, as it is the key to a bright and successful future. At the Ri’ayah Foundation, it is our firm belief that all children deserve a chance to get a quality education, as it is the best way for them to realize their true potential

Our Tree Plantation Programme

A forest-city is a city of the future where humans who live in a world surrounded by high tech and the typical city rat race, can experience a connection with nature and greenery. In a forest-city, we define “growth” as a combination and symbiosis of ecological, communal and economical systems that work in harmony to communicate, nourish, and support our relationship with the earth.
